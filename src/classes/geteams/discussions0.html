Best Marksman character for a beginner?

By: Alexander Reyes
So, I just installed Mobile Legends (ML) as I am not that into mobile games (Let's Genshin, mga dudeparechongs!). Please recommend some marksman that is easy to use for a beginner like me.

Harry Potter
When I was a beginner, I used Layla. She takes a while to get used to since she dies quickly if not used properly (just like most marksman). But, she's actually strong for a free character. Also, take note that marksman (marksmen?) has a low hp, which is why they are usually covered by tanks.
